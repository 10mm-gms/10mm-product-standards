# Story development or small change

Follow these steps if you are developing new functionality to meet a story.  At all points in this workflow, when you move between items in the list output to the chat what workflow you are running and what step you are on. Maintain a checklist of the workflow steps you have completed and the steps you have not completed. 

1. Use implementation.md and design.md in the epic folder to develop the code for the functionality for this story. Ensure you stick to the relevant standards in standards.md in the epic folder.
2. Use implementation.md and design.md in the epic folder to develop the code for the tests for this story. Ensure you stick to the relevant standards in standards.md in the epic folder.
